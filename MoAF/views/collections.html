Legal Tender Notes
  • Issued1862–1971
  • Denominations$10, $20, $50, $100, $500, $1,000
  • Backed byFull faith and Credit of the US Government
Legal Tender Notes, or United States Notes, were released over eight issues between 1862 and 1967. These notes are considered the original “greenbacks” due to the bright green ink used on the backs. While these notes were not backed by specie, they could be traded for gold at a discounted rate before 1879, at which time they were convertible to gold at its current par value.
